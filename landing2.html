Farm Raised:

Our tilapia grow naturally in large floating pens in vast, untainted lakes in Mexico, Honduras and Indonesia. Just like fish in the wild, they swim without crowding against water currents to grow strong and healthy, but without the health risks wild fish face. For optimal health and development, our tilapia are fed a controlled, nutritious and floating grain-based diet. Our quality feed is produced onsite and consists consists of beans, cereals and grains. Our method for raising tilapia ensure our fish do not eat the algae, mud or shallow-pond waste wild-caught fish are exposed to.
